About Me

I was born and raised in a small town in Telangana, India. My childhood was filled with curiosity about nature, farming, and science, which led me to pursue a degree in Agriculture.

Education:

  • PGDM in Agribusiness Management – pursuing at Institute of Agribusiness Management, Chevella, Telangana.
  • B.Sc. (Agriculture) – completed from Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU).
  • Completed various internships focusing on agricultural value chains, digital marketing, and rural development.

Outside academics, I enjoy learning about agri-startups, entrepreneurship, and sustainable farming methods.

My Academic Project

As part of my coursework, I have worked on projects like “Digitalization in Agricultural Marketing” and “Establishment of Vermicompost Unit.” These projects helped me understand the importance of innovation, marketing, and sustainability in the agri-sector.

I also developed a simple “Redgram Crop Information System” using MS Access and website integration, which aims to provide basic crop and pest management details to farmers.
